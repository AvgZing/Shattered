Shattered

Welcome to a shattered and broken land.

Eternal night has come, and the beasts are scurrying. Many kingdoms of old of fallen prey to the night. Venture out into the dark and collect loot, treasures, and fortunes, in the hopes of one day going out and making your own beacon of hope for humanity. Together, we can restore light to the land... Good luck.
